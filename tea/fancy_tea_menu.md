Fancy Tea
=========

#### Food
In overlapping courses
- Cup cauliflower soup
- Tea sandwiches (two of each)
  - Cucumber and cream cheese
  - Pear and brie
  - Egg salad
- Cut fruit and vanilla whipped cream
- Toasted crumpet
- Pumpkin scones (two mini scones)
- Rosemary shortbread cookie

#### Spreads
- Lemon curd
- Devon cream
- Raspberry jam

#### Tea selection
- Rose congou
- Earl gray
- Darjeeling
