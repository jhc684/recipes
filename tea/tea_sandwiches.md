Tea Sandwiches
==============

Use soft white bread. Tea sandwiches should be simple, savory, and light. Cut them into triangles or don't bother making them at all. 

Vegetarian
----------
- Cream cheese and cucumber
- Pesto and mozzarella
- Egg salad
- Cheese and chutney
- Pear and brie

Vegan
-----
- Hummus and cucumber
